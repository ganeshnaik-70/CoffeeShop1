Esay Steps to make a coffe.

  • Step 1

    Always grind your beans as close to the brew time as possible burr or mill grinder is best because the coffee is ground to a consistent size The size of the grind is HUGELY important If your coffee taste bitter, the grind is too fine If your coffee taste flat, the grind might be too coarse.

  • Step 2

    Use filtered or bottled water if your tap water is not good or has a strong odor or taste maintain a water temperature between 195 to 205 degrees Fahrenheit for optimal extraction. Colder water will result in flat while water that is too hot will also cause a loss of quality in the taste of the coffee

  • Step 3

    Then begin to stir briskly and beat coffee for 3 to 4 minutes. You can take a break after 1 to 2 minutes and then continue if your hands start aching. Beat the coffee till its color lightens and you see a frothy layer on top

  • Step 4

    Now shake the jar vigorously for 1 to 2 minutes. Alternatively, to get the froth on milk, you can also use a handheld frother. Milk can be blended in a blender also. There should be a good layer of frothy foam on the milk.
